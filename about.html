I'm a Strategic Business Finance Partner who believes that great finance isn't just about numbers — it's about translating data into decisions that move businesses forward.

Currently at BRND.ME, I manage a ₹250Cr annual portfolio spanning 7 brands, 4 geographies, and multiple business models (B2B, B2C, D2C). My work sits at the intersection of financial rigor and commercial strategy — from building the Annual Operating Plan to designing inventory models that keep margins healthy.

I started my journey at PES University where I led Arthasansadha — the finance club — organizing national and intercollege events that made finance accessible and exciting. That same mission drives my work today: making complex financial data simple, visual, and actionable.

Beyond spreadsheets, I'm passionate about predictive analytics, Power BI dashboards, and exploring how data science intersects with modern finance.
